Welcome to Kisaragi

A town where every petal carries a memory

In the coastal town of Kisaragi, sakura trees bloom year-round, and each falling petal carries echoes of forgotten emotions. You are Hana, a teenage delivery girl who discovers that every parcel she delivers holds more than just contents — it holds the unspoken feelings of the people around her.

Ride your charming scooter through winding streets, make meaningful choices, and unlock the Petal Echo — a mystical ability that lets you experience the emotional memories tied to each delivery.

Gameplay

More than just a delivery

Discover a world brimming with emotion, mystery, and gentle wonder.

Petal Echo

Touch a parcel and experience the emotional memories woven into it. Every delivery reveals fragments of untold stories through a mystical resonance of cherry blossom petals.

Choices that Matter

Your words shape relationships and destinies. Every conversation carries weight, and multiple branching paths lead to seven unique endings.

Open-World Exploration

Ride Hana's adorable scooter through winding streets, hidden alleys, seaside roads, and mountain trails. Discover secrets tucked into every corner of Kisaragi.

Cozy Activities

Between deliveries, tend a small garden, cook regional recipes, customize your scooter, collect petal memories, and enjoy peaceful moments in this gentle world.

Signature Mechanic

The Petal Echo

When Hana touches a parcel, cherry blossom petals swirl with light, revealing emotional memories tied to the package. You will experience fragments of joy, sorrow, longing, and love — guiding you to make choices that can heal or deepen the bonds between Kisaragi's residents.

  • Emotional memory fragments
  • Story-driven puzzle solving
  • Relationship-altering revelations
  • Multiple interpretation paths
